About Rev Christ Street Ministry

Rev Christ Street Ministry operates as a California Nonprofit Public Benefit Corporation and a recognized 501(c)(3) with EIN 86-3870767.

Specific purpose: provide outreach services to homeless individuals (men, women, and children) by offering clean clothes, hygiene resources and products, food, and community‑building support; we also provide clean towels, undergarments, toiletries, personal care items, clothing, laundry vouchers, masks, and hand sanitizer; offer access to safe, dignified showers; and connect people with referrals for food, clothing, shelter, and transitional housing. We also support case management, on‑site clinic access, health and mental‑health workshops and screenings, educational services, computer literacy, senior care services, government benefits, social services advocacy, support groups, job counseling, and self‑expression classes. Our program aims to improve life management skills and reduce unemployment for low‑income and/or homeless individuals and families in Los Angeles.

  • No substantial part of our activities involves lobbying or political campaigning.
  • Assets are irrevocably dedicated to charitable purposes; upon dissolution, assets will transfer to a 501(c)(3) with a similar mission.
  • Service area: City of Los Angeles and Los Angeles County.
