REV CHRIST STREET MINISTRY

We are a California 501(c)(3) nonprofit public benefit corporation. Our mission is to provide outreach services to homeless individuals (men, women, and children) by offering clean clothes, hygiene resources and products, food, and community‑building support. We walk with people experiencing homelessness in Los Angeles through compassion, dignity, and practical support.

Learn more See programs

What We Do

Immediate relief and long‑term pathways out of homelessness.

  • Showers, clean clothing, hygiene kits & meals
  • Referrals for shelter & transitional housing
  • Case management, health & mental‑health workshops

Basic Needs & Hygiene

Hot showers, clean clothes, toiletries, laundry vouchers, and nutritious meals offered with dignity.

Case Management & Health

Personalized support, on‑site clinic access, health & mental‑health screenings, and benefit advocacy.

Education & Employment

GED prep, job coaching, computer literacy, life‑skills, and referrals to vocational training.
